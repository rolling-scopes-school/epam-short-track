## Set 1

1. What is Angular and what is it used for? What are its main advantages compared to other frameworks?
1. What are components in Angular? Describe their structure and role in building an application.
1. How do you configure a component's selector, template, and styles using the `@Component` decorator?
1. What is the purpose of templates in Angular? What are the different ways to define a template?
1. What are directives in Angular? What is the difference between structural and attribute directives?
1. Compare the traditional structural directives (`*ngIf`, `*ngFor`) with the new control flow syntax (`@if`, `@for`, `@switch`). What are the benefits of the new approach?
1. How do you create a custom directive in Angular? Explain the use of the `@Directive` decorator and the host property for event and property binding.
1. What is interpolation and property binding in Angular templates? Provide examples of usage.
1. What is event binding in Angular? How do you handle user events in templates?
1. Explain the difference between one-way and two-way data binding. How is two-way binding implemented in Angular?
1. How do you use `@Input()` and `@Output()` decorators for component communication?
1. What are Pipes in Angular and what are they used for? Give examples of built-in pipes.
1. How do you create a custom pipe? What is the difference between "pure" and "impure" pipes?
1. What are ViewChild and ContentChild in Angular? How and when would you use them?
1. What is content projection in Angular? How do you use `<ng-content>` for creating reusable components?

## Set 2

1. Explain the structure and purpose of Angular CLI. What are the main commands you know and what are they used for?
1. What is the purpose of the Angular application bootstrap process? Which file is the entry point of an Angular application?
1. What are template reference variables and how do you use them?
1. What is the purpose of the `ngClass` and `ngStyle` directives? Provide examples of their usage.
1. How do you pass data from a parent component to a child component? What about from child to parent?
1. Describe the component lifecycle in Angular. What are the main lifecycle hooks you know and when are they called?
1. What is the difference between constructor and `ngOnInit` in a component?
1. What are NgModules? Explain their purpose and the main properties of the `@NgModule` decorator.
1. Explain the role of `NgModule` in organizing an Angular application. What are declarations, imports, providers, and exports?
1. What are shared modules and feature modules? How do they help organize large applications?
1. What is a standalone component in Angular? How does it differ from a traditional component that uses NgModules?
1. How do `HostBinding` and `HostListener` decorators work? How can the same functionality be achieved using the host property in the `@Directive` or `@Component` decorator?
1. Explain the different types of directives in Angular: component directives, structural directives, and attribute directives.
1. What is the change detection mechanism in Angular? Explain the difference between `Default` and `OnPush` strategies.
1. What is the role of the `providers` array in a component or module? How does dependency injection work at the component level?
